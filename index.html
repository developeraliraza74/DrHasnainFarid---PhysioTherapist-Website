Who Am I

Hi, I'm Dr. Hasnain Farid

I'm a registered Physiotherapist serving at Al-Khidmat Hospital, Sahiwal. For over 7 years, I've dedicated my career to one goal — helping people move better, live pain-free, and get back to what they love.

Whether you're dealing with back pain, a frozen shoulder, a sports injury, or recovering from surgery — I personally assess and design a treatment plan built around your condition.

I also offer home visits for patients who can't make it to the clinic, and specialized care for children with cerebral palsy and elderly patients.

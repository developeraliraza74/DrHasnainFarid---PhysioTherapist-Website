Who We Are

About Dr. Husnain Fareed

Dr. Husnain Fareed is a highly skilled and registered physiotherapist practicing at Al Khidmat Center in Sahiwal. With extensive experience in treating various musculoskeletal conditions, Dr. Fareed provides exceptional care tailored to each patient's needs.

He specializes in treating back pain, neck pain, sports injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation, and other physiotherapy needs. His patient-centered approach ensures optimal recovery and improved quality of life.
